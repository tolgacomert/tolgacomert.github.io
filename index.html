About Me

My name is Tolga, I am 25 years old and born and raised in Utrecht. While growing up, people called me a 'knowledge sponge': I was capable of remembering lots of, mostly, irrelevant information, such as who the highest goal scorer was in the Serie A in 1997 (Filippo Inzaghi!). In the present time, I am still the same person, however, I now use my qualities for engineering!

I have studied Civil and Hydraulic Engineering at Delft University of Technology from 2013-2019. I got to learn more about data science in my free time and when I realised that it possesses lots of potential to shape our future, I decided to study Data Science at Tilburg University in 2020. In 2019, I joined Fugro, the world's leader in geo-data, as an International Management Trainee. Next to learning more about the technical skills we have as engineers, I am also actively involved in strategic advices to our business lines, something I want to explore more throughout my career.

You can learn more about my experiences in my CV or on my LinkedIn page.
